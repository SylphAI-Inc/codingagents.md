## What It Does

Windsurf is an AI-powered IDE featuring Cascade — an agentic system that maintains deep awareness of your codebase and can execute multi-step coding workflows.

## Key Strengths

- **Cascade**: Agentic flow that understands context deeply
- **Context engine**: Indexes and understands your full codebase
- **Multi-model**: Supports multiple AI providers
- **Affordable**: Competitive pricing with free tier

## Best For

- Developers wanting an AI IDE at a lower price point
- Projects requiring deep codebase awareness
- Teams migrating from VS Code
